About us



We are a society which aims to provide information, guidance and tips for women trying to enter the world of tech. In our mission, we work with international charities to make this career possible for women from all backgrounds. Our website will offer you the most up-to-date career opportunities, news articles and inspirational stories to help you get your dream job.
